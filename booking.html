ALMA DANCE IS MOVING TO A NEW DANCE STUDIO IN BORNHEIM!

As of March, all our classes will take place in new spaces with two rooms, nice mirrors, cool light and even a kitchen! It means that we will have more comfort in our classes, more space for new exciting projects, a nicer atmosphere and more opportunities to connect with you!

New address: Alt-Bornheim 35.

  • Why?: Our aim has always been to offer you not only high quality teaching and good learning opportunities but also a space where a community of like-minded people from all over the world can feel welcome and connect. We believe that our new school will help us get closer to achieving our goal.
